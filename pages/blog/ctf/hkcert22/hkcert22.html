What is HKCert22?

HKCERT CTF 2022 is a Capture The Flag competition held by the Hong Kong Computer Emergency Response Team Coordination Centre (HKCERT) and the Hong Kong Productivity Council (HKPC), and aims to increase awareness of cyber security.




Our Team:

Our team consist of 4 members, Kapakki Lo (me), Bill Chan, Steven Suen and Jimmy Yau.

We had no prior experience with hacking, so technically this was our first time approaching and learning hacking in the cyber security field, and we were basically solving and learning on the fly.

Despite our lack of experience, we still managed to get on the leaderboard in the Secondary Division, and end with a result of [Secondary #4] and [All #29], with 1513 Total Score.




About:

This blog page is about some of the write-ups on the solutions we have used in this time's CTF.

This is our first time participating in a CTF event, and we have learned a lot about cyber security.  We are definitely looking forward for other competitions or events similar to this one!
